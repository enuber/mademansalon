About Made Man

We are all familiar with being faced with choices when shopping. We know which stores carry what products and, the quality of the products those places will carry. When faced with even simple choices there are many different brands to choose from; store name to high-end and, if one is honest with them selves, there are times when this actually matters. Whether it's the quality of a food item, or the quality of a piece of fabric your hair deserves the same consideration.

Yelena has been cutting hair for more than fifteen years and, it is truly her passion. She has built up a high-end clientele of executives, CEOs, lawyers and, has clients that still come to her from other countries and states when visiting the area. The reason for this is simple; it is rare to find someone that is a true master stylist.

In continuing her education about hair she has also become a hair loss specialist having studied and become certifed in Master Alopecia by the National Hair Loss Association. You can get a free consultation and ask her questions as well as suggestions in regards to your own circumstances.

Her main focus is men’s hair whether it is cutting and styling, coloring or doing grey blending; men’s hair is different than woman’s and, is really a specialty. Along with regular services that you would expect from a hair stylist, she also does nose, ear and eyebrow waxing as well as special skin and scalp treatments to keep you looking your best.

Having her own salon has been a dream for her and, it shows in all the details she has used to create her own place. The line of products she carries are, her own choices; products she uses regularly and believes in. Her salon was designed as a haven of masculinity and, reflects her own thoughtfulness for her clients. Sit in the comfort of a leather winged back chair while enjoying an espresso, sipping on a glass of wine or having a beer. You can watch TV, ask Amazon's Alexa, which is hooked up to a Marshall speaker, to play music that you want to hear, or read from a variety of current magazines.
